Publish or Perish is a free software for researchers to prepare for their tenure packet or performance appraisal.

This software allows users to see their research impact.

Other functions include:

  • Provides guidance in selecting appropriate journals for article submission

  • Facilitates the process of conducting comprehensive literature reviews and performing bibliometric research

  • Aids in crafting laudatios or obituaries

  • Enables research on notable academic figures
